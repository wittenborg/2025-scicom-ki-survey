Data protection notice:

The survey is conducted via LimeSurvey, a service of Leibniz Universität Hannover. The data is stored securely on the servers there and deleted 3 months after the end of the survey.

The survey is anonymous; no personal data is collected. This means that your answers cannot be traced back to you. You can stop at any time, but you cannot withdraw completed surveys, as the anonymization means that it is not possible to identify which contribution was made by you.

The results of the survey will be used exclusively for the development of the platform, the Master's thesis and scientific publications. All data used and shared for this purpose is anonymized.

If you have any further questions, please contact niklas.stehr@stud.uni-hannover.de or tim.wittenborg@l3s.uni-hannover.de.
